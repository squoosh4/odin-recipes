mmmm.. delicious pasta recipe

  1. buy pasta from store
  2. buy tomato sauce from store
  3. get pot and fill it with water
  4. start boiling water
  5. when the water reaches a rolling boil, put the pasta in
  6. let pasta cook
  7. season pasta while cooking in the water for more flavor
  8. drain water
  9. if you will be adding the sauce after you put it on the plate then do that, otherwise mix the sauce in the pot with the pasta (be generous with the sauce)
  10. dig in

now you know how to make pasta
